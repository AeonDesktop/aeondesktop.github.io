Features At a Glance

Distrobox
Aeon ships with Distrobox configured to launch Tumbleweed containers perfect for your development tools and projects.
Flatpak Support
Flatpak is set up ready for you to find your favourite apps from flathub.
A Minimal Yet Functional Base
The base system is keps small and simple, but with everthing you need for getting straight on with your tasks - programming, gaming, media production and clerical work.
Automated Maintenance
Daily transactional updates happen in the background without any need to disturb you. Utilising the power of btrfs snapshots, your running system is not affected until you reboot, keeping your workday consistent and avoiding any unexpected restarts of your apps.

Who is Aeon For?

Aeon is perfect for the "lazy developer" who prefers to spend their time getting work done while letting their desktop take care of itself. The OS is static, automated and reliable, letting you focus on your apps and work.
Aeon has a clear vision of a reliable and consistent experience across all installs, which may not be the perfect environment for a hardcore system-tinkerer - there's still Tumbleweed and Leap for scratching that itch.
